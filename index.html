About Ruvimbo

Ruvimbo Special School is a school for children with disabilities in Zimbabwe. It was founded in 1971 and currently has an enrollment of 101 children. The school caters for children with a variety of disabilities, including autism, Down syndrome, microcephaly, mental retardation, cerebral palsy, hyperactivity, and epilepsy.

What we offer

The school provides a great learning environment for children with disabilities. The teaching staff is experienced and dedicated, and the school offers a variety of programs and services to meet the needs of its students.
These programs include:

Academic Instruction

Speech Therapy

Physical Therapy

Occupational Therapy
